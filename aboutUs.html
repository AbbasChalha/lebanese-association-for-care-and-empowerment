About Us

The Lebanese Association for Care and Empowerment (LACE)

is defined as non-profit organization that focuses primarily on doing all non-profit activities for community reform. The main goal for this organization is to provide assistance to people, especially the poor class, by supporting them with money, food and clothes, which will be provided by donors.

LACE aims to provide its services and aid to people regardless of their sectarian, political or religious affiliations so that people may obtain and enjoy their rights. The idea of racial and sect-based discrimination does not exist at LACE and we follow a purely humanitarian path.
