Lebanese Association for Care and Empowerment

The Lebanese association for care and empowerment "LACE" site is defined as non-profit organization that focuses primarily on doing all non-profit activities for community reform.

The main goal for this organization is to provide assistance to people, especially the poor class, by supporting them with money, food and clothes, which will be provided by donors.
